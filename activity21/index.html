Activity 21: Technical Research

Overview

For this assignment, I selected three coffee shop websites with similar audiences and purposes: Starbucks, Dutch Bros, and Peet’s Coffee. I used four online testing tools to analyze their website performance and security: Google PageSpeed Insights, GTmetrix, Pingdom Website Speed Test, and Sucuri SiteCheck. Below is the collected technical data for each website.

  • Starbucks — https://www.starbucks.com
  • Dutch Bros — https://www.dutchbros.com
  • Peet’s Coffee — https://www.peets.com
