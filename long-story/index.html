A Day in the Life

Follow a student’s quiet morning to a colorful night of creativity.

Morning – Waking Up

The light peeks through the dorm window. A new day begins with coffee and quiet thoughts.

Afternoon – On Campus

Walking across campus, the air buzzes with ideas and laughter. Deadlines, caffeine, and design critiques fill the hours.

Evening – Gallery Opening

Soft lights, printed posters, and friends gathered around. The work finally comes to life.

Night – Reflection

Back in bed, the city hums outside. The day fades into calm and inspiration for tomorrow.
