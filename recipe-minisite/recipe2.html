Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta

Total: ~25 min Active: 25 min Yield: 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 180 g spaghetti (or linguine)
  • 250 g shrimp, peeled & deveined
  • 3 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • ½ lemon — zest & juice
  • Salt & black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp chopped parsley

Directions

  1. Cook pasta: Boil in salted water until al dente; reserve ½ cup cooking water.
  2. Sear shrimp: Pat dry, season with salt/pepper. Sauté in 1 Tbsp oil + 1 Tbsp butter, 1–2 min/side; remove.
  3. Make sauce: Add remaining oil; cook garlic and pepper flakes ~30 sec. Stir in remaining butter, lemon zest & juice.
  4. Toss: Add pasta, splash of pasta water, and shrimp. Toss to coat; adjust with more water if needed.
  5. Finish: Stir in parsley; taste and adjust salt/pepper. Serve hot.
