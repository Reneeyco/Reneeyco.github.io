Matcha Latte

Total: 5–7 min Active: 5 min Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp ceremonial-grade matcha
  • 60 ml hot water (≈80 °C / 176 °F)
  • 200 ml milk (dairy or oat/almond)
  • Honey or syrup, to taste (optional)
  • Ice (for iced version)

Directions

  1. Sift: Sift matcha into a bowl to remove clumps.
  2. Whisk: Add hot water; whisk in a fast “W” motion until frothy.
  3. Milk: Warm and froth milk (or keep cold for iced). Sweeten if desired.
  4. Combine: Pour matcha into milk. For iced, add ice to a glass, milk, then top with whisked matcha.
  5. Serve: Enjoy immediately; stir gently before sipping.
