Korean Steamed Egg

Korean steamed egg(Gyeran-jjim) is a fluffy, savory custard cooked withh broth, often served in a hot stone bowl with green onions or sesame oil.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup water or chicken or vegetabel broth
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 green onion, chopped
