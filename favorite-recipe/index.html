Korean Steamed Egg

Korean steamed egg(Gyeran-jjim) is a fluffy, savory custard cooked withh broth, often served in a hot stone bowl with green onions or sesame oil.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup water or chicken or vegetabel broth
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 green onion, chopped

Directions

  1. Whisk eggs, broth, and salt in a bowl until well mixed.
  2. Pour into a small pot or heat-safe bowl.
  3. Cook over low heat, stirring gently until it starts to thicken, then cover and steam for 5 to 7 minutes until fluffy.
  4. Drizzle sesame oil and sprinkle green onion on top before serving.
