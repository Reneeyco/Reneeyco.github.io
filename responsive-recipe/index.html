Japanese Soufflé Pancakes

Total: ~35 min Active: 25 min Yield: 1–2 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs (50 g each w/o shell)
  • 1½ Tbsp whole milk
  • ¼ tsp pure vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup cake flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp neutral oil (for greasing the pan)
  • 2 Tbsp water (for steaming)

Directions

  1. Make yolk batter: Whisk yolks, milk, and vanilla. Sift in cake flour + baking powder; mix until smooth.
  2. Whip meringue: Beat whites until foamy; add sugar gradually; whip to glossy medium peaks.
  3. Fold: Whisk in ⅓ of meringue to loosen; gently fold remaining meringue in two parts until just combined.
  4. Portion: Lightly oil a nonstick pan, heat on low. Scoop tall mounds of batter; add more on top after 1 min.
  5. Steam-cook: Add ~1 Tbsp water to the pan (not on pancakes), cover, and cook 6–7 minutes.
  6. Flip & finish: Flip carefully, add more water, cover, and cook another 4–5 minutes until jiggly. Serve immediately.
